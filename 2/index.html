Reflection: What I Learned

This project helped me understand the importance of frequency analysis in image processing. I learned how to use filters for edge detection, how Gaussian smoothing improves robustness, and how multiresolution techniques enable seamless blending. The hybrid image experiments also revealed how human perception interprets different frequencies at different viewing distances.
